<p><p>The acne medication market is primarily divided into two categories: topical and oral medications. Topical medications include creams, gels, and lotions that are applied directly to the skin, targeting localized acne lesions and reducing inflammation. Common ingredients include benzoyl peroxide and salicylic acid. Oral medications, on the other hand, include systemic treatments such as antibiotics and hormonal therapies that address acne from within the body, often used in more severe cases. Both types aim to improve skin health and prevent future breakouts.</p></p>
