<p><p>Breast lesion guidance systems encompass various technologies for precise localization during breast surgery. Wire-Guided Localization (WGL) involves using a thin wire inserted into the lesion to guide surgeons. Magnetic tracers utilize magnetically injected substances to mark lesions, enhancing visibility. Electromagnetic reflectors employ electromagnetic fields to assist in targeting specific areas. Other methods may include ultrasound or optical technologies, providing alternative solutions for pinpointing lesions, ultimately improving surgical accuracy and patient outcomes in breast cancer treatment.</p></p>
