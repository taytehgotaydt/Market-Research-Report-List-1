<p><p>The 3D hydrogels in cell culture market can be categorized into scaffold-free and scaffold-based types. Scaffold-free hydrogels allow cells to self-assemble into tissue-like structures, promoting natural growth and functionality. In contrast, scaffold-based hydrogels provide a supportive framework for cells, enhancing structural stability and guiding tissue formation. Both types are utilized in regenerative medicine and drug testing, offering versatile platforms for mimicking the extracellular matrix and improving cell behavior in vitro.</p></p>
